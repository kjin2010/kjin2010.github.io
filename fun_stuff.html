Let's get this bread

Join me and my pet sourdough starter, Karl, as we try to master the art of bread baking. Documenting our journey to look back on, the results of each oven burn and every spill of flour will be on full display. And so what if not every boule turns out perfect. At least we got some nice (and some not so nice) pictures out of it.

And carbs. Lots of carbs.
